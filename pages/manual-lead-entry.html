At the Door Process

1 Always give them the hanger/flyer

Hand it to them as soon as they open. It gives them our services, phone number, website, and a reminder for later.

2 Yes, we need email, phone, & addy

Submit all info they are willing to give into the form.

  • Name
  • Phone
  • Email
  • Address
  • Project description
  • Measurements/notes
  • Before pictures when possible

"What's a good phone number to contact you?"

"What's a good email we can send the estimate to?"

"And what is the address here?"

"Awesome, we will text you from our number within about 1 hour and email you that estimate within 24 hours."

Put all of it in the form and hit submit.

Once the form is submitted, the lead is in the company system. Do not keep a separate side list on your phone.

3 No info on your personal phone

Real lead info goes in the company system, not personal notes, contacts, texts, screenshots, or memory.

When You Get Home Process

1 Text within an hour

Text from Google Voice: (801) 477-7526. Do not use your personal number.

Hi, this is ___ from A Team. We talked about ____ for you. Here's our number to contact us and we will get your estimate to you within the next day or so. Thanks!

After the text, check the Trello card that was created from the form and make sure it is in the right column, has the right label/person assigned, and has enough info to estimate.

Note: This estimating workflow is still a work in progress. For now, use your best judgment, get manager help when needed, and make sure every official estimate still goes through QBO before being sent to the customer.

2 Build the estimate

Use the form notes, photos, measurements, and project details to build a real estimate.

Before sending, make sure you understand:

  • What work they want done
  • Where the project is
  • What materials may be needed
  • Whether photos/measurements are enough
  • Whether a walkthrough is needed before final pricing
  • Whether the job is worth scheduling soon or needs manager review

If the info is incomplete, text or call from Google Voice and ask for what you need.

Do not guess blindly if the missing info could change the price a lot.

Use this message if needed:

Thanks again. Before we send the estimate, could you send a couple pictures and any rough measurements you have? That will help us price it more accurately.

If the scope is too unclear, move the Trello card to the appropriate follow-up/walkthrough column instead of forcing an estimate.

3 Send the estimate within 24 hours via QBO

You or your manager should send the official estimate through QuickBooks Online within 24 hours whenever possible.

Send the estimate through both:

  • The standard QBO email
  • Download the QBO PDF using "Print" and text it from Google Voice

No random text estimates. No screenshots as the official estimate. No unofficial-only PDFs. No verbal-only pricing.

The QBO estimate is the official estimate. The texted duplicate PDF is just an extra way to make sure they actually see it.

The form starts the company workflow automatically. The real system is:
Lead form -> Company system -> Trello -> Google Voice follow-up -> QBO estimate
