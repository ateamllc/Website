Twilio Campaign Privacy Policy

SMS privacy for customers

How A Team Property Improvement LLC handles phone numbers and SMS opt-in information.

Last updated: July 17, 2026

This Twilio Campaign Privacy Policy applies specifically to SMS/text messaging campaigns operated by A Team Property Improvement LLC for customers and prospective customers.

Information collected for SMS campaigns

  • Your mobile phone number.
  • Your name, email address, service address, and project details when you provide them.
  • Your SMS opt-in status, message preferences, and communication history with us.
  • Information needed to respond to estimate requests, scheduling questions, service updates, and customer support messages.

How SMS information is used

We use SMS campaign information to send estimate follow-ups, appointment reminders, scheduling updates, project updates, customer support replies, and service-related follow-up for A Team Property Improvement LLC.

No selling or marketing sharing

SMS opt-in information and phone numbers collected for SMS purposes are not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for their marketing or promotional purposes.

We may share limited information with service providers, including communications platforms such as Twilio, only as needed to deliver messages, operate our business, comply with law, or protect our rights.

Your SMS choices

You can opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP, call (801) 477-7526, or contact us through the online contact form.

Data retention

We keep SMS campaign information only as long as needed to provide services, maintain business records, comply with legal requirements, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

For message frequency, carrier cost disclosures, HELP instructions, and STOP opt-out details, review our SMS Terms.
