Different Methods of Repairing Old Fences

Pick the right repair method for Utah wind, sprinklers, and sun before you spend on a full replacement.

Different Methods of Repairing Old Fences

1) Replace individual pickets

The most common fix when boards cup or split. Match thickness and width so the fence stays level.

2) Replace failing posts

Two options:

  • Full removal and new concrete set.
  • Post-saver bracket system to avoid digging out the old footing.

3) Reinforce a leaning fence

  • Add a new post beside the old to stand the section back up.
  • Or reset the entire section with new footings if multiple spans lean.

4) Replace rails only

Rails rot or split long before pickets. Swapping rails can buy years of life when pickets are still solid.

If more than 30% of posts are gone, replacement is usually cheaper than piecemeal repairs.
