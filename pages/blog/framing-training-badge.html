Framing work has to be square, plumb, straight, and structurally sensible. This badge teaches the vocabulary and field judgment new team members need before they help with walls, openings, floors, roof framing, repairs, or load-bearing changes.

A Team standard: framing should give drywall, trim, doors, windows, and structural loads a clean place to land.
