Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide free estimates?
Yes — we offer free consultations and estimates for most projects. We can usually provide a range remotely and follow up with an on-site visit.
Are you licensed and insured?
Absolutely. We are fully licensed and insured in the State of Utah and maintain coverage that protects both your property and our team.
What areas do you serve?
Provo, Orem, Springville, Spanish Fork, Mapleton, Payson, and surrounding Utah County neighborhoods.
What types of projects do you take on?
Small to mid-sized remodels, fences, decks, painting, drywall, finish work, and general handyman punch lists. If it's outside our wheelhouse, we'll let you know.
How soon can you start?
We keep a flexible schedule to prioritize urgent issues. Contact us with your target timeline and we'll let you know the soonest availability.
