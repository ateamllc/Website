Privacy Policy

Your information is in good hands

We collect the bare minimum needed to provide estimates and keep your project moving.

Last updated: July 17, 2026

A Team Property Improvement LLC respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect customer contact information, including information used for SMS/text messaging.

Information we collect

  • Contact information you provide when requesting an estimate, contacting us, booking service, or opting in to text messages, such as your name, phone number, email address, and service address.
  • Project details you share through forms, phone calls, text messages, email, photos, or in-person conversations.
  • Communication records related to estimates, scheduling, service updates, invoices, customer support, and follow-up.
  • Basic website usage information from cookies, analytics, browser data, and similar technologies used to understand and improve our website.

How we use information

We use customer information to respond to inquiries, prepare estimates, schedule work, provide handyman, repair, painting, fencing, and property improvement services, send appointment or project updates, process payments, provide customer support, and improve our website and services.

SMS/text messaging

If you provide your phone number and opt in to receive text messages from A Team Property Improvement LLC, we may send messages related to estimates, scheduling, appointment reminders, project updates, customer support, and service follow-up.

SMS opt-in information and mobile phone numbers collected for SMS purposes are not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for their marketing or promotional purposes. We may share information only with service providers who help us deliver messages or operate our business, and only as needed to provide those services.

Message frequency varies based on your requests, scheduled services, active projects, and customer support needs. Message and data rates may apply.

Third-party services

We may use trusted third-party services for website hosting, analytics, maps, payment processing, communications, scheduling, customer management, and similar business operations. These providers are allowed to use information only as needed to provide services to us or as required by law.

How we protect information

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information. No website, transmission, or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure, but we work to keep customer information limited, protected, and used only for appropriate business purposes.

Your choices

You may request that we update or delete your personal information by using the contact form or calling us. You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. For help by text, reply HELP.

We will only retain information as long as necessary to provide services or comply with legal requirements.
